## [A model for COVID-19 with isolation, quarantine and testing as control measures](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.epidem.2021.100437)

This paper deals with a deterministic model based on ordinary differential equations for COVID-19 to understand non-pharmaceutical control measures, such as isolation, quarantine, and testing.

Available online 21 january 2021 in Epidemics Journal.
