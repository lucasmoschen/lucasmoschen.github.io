Biography

I am an Undergraduate Student at School of Applied Mathematics (EMAp), at the Getulio Vargas Foundation (FGV) and a huge fan of the Star Wars franchise.

I started my life in mathematics at a national math olympics (OBMEP). After receiving my first medal, I was introduced to a junior scientific initiation (PIC), focused on algebra, number theory and geometry, which contributed to understand my passion for the topic. I participated in this program for 4 years.

This has opened up several opportunities, such as studying mathematics at the university. In particular, in 2018, I started studying at FGV where I am now in my last year of graduation. In 2019, I jointly begun taking master's classes encouraged by the Scientific Initiation and Master's Program (PICME) . This led me to anticipate the master's degree at the same institution. Besides that, I participate in a Scientific Initiation about Optimal Control Theory guided by Prof. Maria Soledad Aronna.
